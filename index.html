Featured Indian Recipes

  • Biryani (Hyderabadi)

    Ingredients: Basmati Rice, Chicken, Spices, Saffron

    Instructions: Marinate chicken, layer with partially cooked rice, and cook on low heat until done.

  • Pav Bhaji (Maharashtrian)

    Ingredients: Mixed Vegetables, Butter, Pav Buns, Spices

    Instructions: Boil and mash mixed vegetables, add spices, serve with buttered pav buns.

  • Dosa (South Indian)

    Ingredients: Rice, Urad Dal, Fermented Batter

    Instructions: Heat a skillet, pour batter, and cook until crispy on both sides.

  • Butter Chicken (North Indian)

    Ingredients: Chicken, Butter, Cream, Tomato Sauce

    Instructions: Cook marinated chicken in creamy tomato sauce until tender.

  • Naan (Indian Flatbread)

    Ingredients: Flour, Yeast, Yogurt, Butter

    Instructions: Prepare dough, allow it to rise, roll out, and cook on a tandoor or skillet.

  • Samosa (Indian Snack)

    Ingredients: Flour, Potatoes, Peas, Spices

    Instructions: Prepare a dough, stuff with spiced potatoes and peas, then fry until crispy.

  • Modak (Indian Dessert)

    Ingredients: Rice Flour, Jaggery, Coconut, Cardamom

    Instructions: Prepare dough with rice flour, stuff with jaggery and coconut mixture, and steam until cooked.

  • Jalebi (Indian Dessert)

    Ingredients: Flour, Sugar Syrup, Yogurt

    Instructions: Prepare a fermented batter, fry it in oil, and soak in sugar syrup.
